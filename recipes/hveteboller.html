(Wheat Rolls)

Ingredients

  • 1 cake compressed yeast or 2 1/4 tsp dry yeast
  • 1 cup milk, scalded
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 4 cups flour
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/2 tsp crushed cardamom seeds
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Currants (optional)

Steps

  1. Dissolve yeast in a little warm water.
  2. Sift flour and sugar together and cut in butter.
  3. Add lukewarm milk and yeast, egg, salt, and cardomom - mix thoroughly.
  4. Knead until dough is smooth.
  5. Put dough in greased bowl to rise until double its size.
  6. Knead well, add currants if desired, and shape into small round balls and place on buttered cookie sheet.
  7. Cover rolls and let rise until light.
  8. Bake 10-12 minutes at 375 degrees.
  9. For a golden brown color, glaze tops with beaten egg before baking.
