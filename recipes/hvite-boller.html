Ingredients

  • 2 packages yeast
  • 1 3/4 cups milk, scalded
  • 1 3/4 cups water
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 tsp salt
  • 12 - 13 cups flour

Directions

  1. Dissolve yeast according to directions.
  2. Pour hot milk over butter, then add sugar, salt, and water. When lukewarm, add yeast.
  3. Add enough flour to make a soft sponge, beat well, then add just enough flour so dough can be easily handled.
  4. Knead well and put in greased bowl to rise. When double in size knead down and let rise again until almost double.
  5. Shape into rolls and place in pans to rise.
  6. Bake at 375 degrees for 25-30 minutes.
