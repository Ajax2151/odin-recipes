Ingredients

    Cake

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 1/4 cups flour
  • 2 eggs, separated
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt

    • Topping

  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup chopped pecans

Directions

  1. Cream butter and sugar.
  2. Sift flour with salt.
  3. Add egg yolks to milk.
  4. Add liquid and dry ingredients to butter and sugar alternately. Fold in egg whites last.
  5. Pour into well-greased baking pan.
  6. Mix topping ingredients together, sprinkle over top.
  7. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
