Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup rich milk or a mix of 1/2 cup whole milk and 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 egg
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • 5 3/4 cups flour
  • 10 cardamom seeds, crushed
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Directions

  1. Cream butter and sugar, add slightly beaten egg and milk.
  2. Sift dry ingredients together and add to wet ingredients. Mix as little as possible.
  3. Turn dough out on floured board and shape into small buns.
  4. Bake at 400 degrees for about 10 minutes.
  5. Cut each roll in half and return to oven.
  6. Bake at 300 degrees until rusks are light brown.
