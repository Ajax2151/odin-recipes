Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup whipping cream
  • 1 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 tsp soda
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg

Steps

  1. Beat eggs well.
  2. Add sugar and butter.
  3. Fold in whipped cream.
  4. Add buttermilk and flour alternately.
  5. Nutmeg may be used for flavoring, or vanilla or 1/4 tsp finely crushed cardamom seeds.
  6. Bake in Krum Kaka iron.
