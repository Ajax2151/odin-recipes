Ingredients

  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup dark syrup
  • 3 eggs, well beaten
  • 4 Tbsp whipping cream
  • 4 1/2 cups flour
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 3/4 tsp ginger
  • 3/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp cloves
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Boil sugar, syrup, adn butter together for 5 minutes, coll and add eggs and cream.
  2. Sift dry ingredients and add.
  3. Chill thoroughly, roll out dough, and cut as desired.
  4. Bake 8-10 minutes at 400 degrees.
