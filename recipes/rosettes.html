Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 cup milk

Steps

  1. Beat eggs, sugar, and salt slightly.
  2. Add milk and flour, beat until smooth.
  3. Bake with a rosette iron.
  4. Heat iron well in boiling oil or fat adn dip into the batter, being careful not to let batter run over the form.
  5. Dip the iron with batter sticking to it into the hot fat until nicely browned.
  6. Omit the sugar in batter if used for anything but cake.
  7. When serving, sprinkle with powdered sugar.
